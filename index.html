5-STAR Lake Front Minnesota Vacation Homes and Cabins for ALL Occasions Year Round

Surrounded by awe inspiring panoramic views of deep greens and blue landscapes, combined with unspoiled natural beauty of birch, spruce, maples, vibrant sunsets and calm summer breezes, this beautiful lake front resort, originally established in 1932, is proud to be offering a truly unique hi-end boutique resort experience, like no other in Minnesota. This BRAND NEW 5-STAR ALL-INCLUSIVE resort, now offers luxury homes and cabin accommodations overlooking Blackduck Lake and the “lazy” knee deep Blackduck River, just minutes north of Bemidji, 3 ½ hours north of Minneapolis, 3 hours east of Fargo and 1 hour south of Canada. Once you’ve set foot at White Birch Resort, it is easy to see why this picturesque destination has always been proud to lure guests to a multi-generational tradition, as demonstrated by repeat guests. Guest are no longer coming to just fulfill dreams of fishing at an iconic “Premier Top 10 Best” fishing camp. WBR is a northern slice of paradise for vacationers to enjoy!
